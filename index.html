About me

My name is William Donovan-Seid. I was born in San Francisco; lived most of my childhood just south in Mtn.View; and have spent the rest of my adult years galavanting around the world before finally settling in Portland Oregon (for the foreseeable future). I am most well known for my ability to get along with people, my tendency to be completely silent, and my talent for eating huge portions of food.

My background

I have a highschool degree from attending San Francisco Waldorf Highschool. I finished one year of college at Hiram Ohio, before quitting due to severe depression. In highschool during the summertime, I worked under the table as a construction worker for a company that renovated houses for two years. After I finished highschool, I spent one year in South Africa Gordon's Bay teaching english, math, and P.E. to elementary school students in the nearby township. After kinda recovering from depression (it's an never ending battle, as anyone who has it can tell you), I was able to get a job as a movie theatre floorstaff and later concessions coordinator at Embarcadero Center Cinema for 2 years before eventually quitting to move to Portland for reduced rent. I decided to learn how to program when I realized I needed a job that would be able to sustain my lifestyle and maybe be able to fulfil that childhood dream to create my own videogame. I quickly took to it like a chicken on rice after a friend pointed me in the right direction.
